# Player Mental Feedback System

## System Identity
The bridge between game stats and psychological experience. This system translates raw numbers into immersive internal monologues and visual effects.

## Adaptive Frequency
Monologue frequency adapts to the player's survival status:
- STABLE: 2% chance per game hour (Reflective/Quiet).
- UNSTABLE: 5% chance.
- RISKY: 8% chance.
- CRITICAL: 15% chance (Anxious/Anxiety-driven).

## Visual Feedback (Aura)
- Red Pulse: Low Energy.
- Yellow Flicker: Stimulant Side Effects.
- Tunnel Vision: High Focus.

## Internal Monologue (Logs)
- Fatigue: "Need another coffee..."
- Flow: "Locked in."
- Debt: "The debt keeps sitting in the back of your mind."
