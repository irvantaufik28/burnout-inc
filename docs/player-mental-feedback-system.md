# Player Mental Feedback System

## System Identity
The bridge between game stats and psychological experience. This system translates raw numbers (Energy, Focus, Money) into immersive internal monologues and visual effects.

## Visual Feedback (Aura)
- Red Pulse: Low Energy (Exhaustion).
- Yellow Flicker: Stimulant Side Effects (Wired).
- Tunnel Vision: High Focus / Flow State.

## Internal Monologue (Logs)
Logs are prefixed with [MIND] and represent the player's internal state.
- Fatigue: "Need another coffee..."
- Flow: "Everything suddenly makes sense."
- Financial Anxiety: "The upcoming bills are stressing you out."

## Trigger Logic
Monologues are probability-based and triggered by specific game state conditions (e.g., bills due in < 2 days).
