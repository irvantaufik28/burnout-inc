# Device Maintenance System

## System Identity
Hardware is the freelancer's lifeline. This system ensures that technical problems are not just instant financial drains, but persistent psychological and operational pressures.

## Maintenance Philosophy
Broken devices represent ongoing real-life problems. If you ignore a hardware issue, it doesn't go away—it lingers and restricts your productivity ceiling.

## Focus Cap Penalty
Ignoring device issues applies a penalty to your **Max Focus**.
- Normal: 100% Max Focus.
- Broken Hardware: Restricts the bar (e.g., 85% or 70%).
- Impact: You can still work, but you can never reach full mental comfort or maximum productivity until the repair is settled.

## Escalation Logic
Device issues worsen over time if ignored.
- **Initial:** Base penalty (e.g., -15%).
- **Escalation:** Penalty increases after 7-14 in-game days.
- **Critical:** Potential for temporary work slowdowns or thermal shutdowns.

## Anti-Spam Protection
Hardware issues are rare and grounded.
- **Cooldown:** Minimum 10 in-game days between device damage events.
- **Uniqueness:** You cannot trigger the same device issue if it is already pending.

## Repair Flow
Pending repairs are listed in the **Pending Maintenance Panel**. You can resolve them at any time by paying the repair cost, which immediately restores your Max Focus capacity.
