# Dual Time Scale System

## System Identity
The Dual Time Scale system ensures that Burnout Inc maintains a fast, engaging project progression while preserving a believable and immersive emotional pacing for human needs. It prevents the "30-second sleep cycle" problem.

## Core Philosophy
We compress **Project Time** while stretching **Human Time**.
- **Project Systems:** Progress quickly to keep the management gameplay exciting.
- **Human Systems:** Progress slowly to simulate realistic fatigue and burnout accumulation.

## Time Scales

### 1. Project Time (Fast)
- **Scale:** 1 game hour = 1 real second (at 1x speed).
- **Controls:** Project progress, deadlines, chaos triggers, contract board, and work cycles.
- **Frequency:** Updates every game tick (1 second).

### 2. Human Time (Slow)
- **Scale:** Updates occur every 4 game hours.
- **Controls:** Energy decay, focus drift, stimulant fatigue, and burnout accumulation.
- **Human Fatigue Cycle:** 1 human update = 4 real seconds (at 1x speed).

## Fatigue Rules and Decay

### Contextual Decay Rates (per 4 game hours)
- **Idle State:** Very slow decay (0.5 Energy, 0.8 Focus). Stops at the "Safe Floor".
- **Active Project:** Normal decay (1.5 Energy, 2.0 Focus).
- **Crunch / Stimulant Abuse:** High decay (up to 4.0 Energy, 3.5 Focus).

### The Safe Floor
Idle decay naturally stabilizes around **40-50%**. This ensures that players only collapse from overwork or abuse, not simply from existing.

### Circadian Rhythm Modifier
A lightweight modifier applies during Late Night (00:00 - 05:00):
- **Increased Focus Drain:** +1.0 focus decay.
- **Increased Fatigue:** +0.5 energy decay.

## Psychological Readability
The system integrates with **Mental Feedback** to trigger internal monologues when the player has been awake for long periods or is reaching dangerous fatigue levels.

## UI Integration
The Freelancer Status panel displays a "Fatigue Pressure" indicator (Stable, Tired, Exhausted) based on current energy levels.
