# Economy System: Capital and Survival

## System Identity
The economy of Burnout Inc is designed to create a sense of persistent, realistic pressure.

## Financial Layers
1. Daily Expenses: $40 for food and survival.
2. Weekly Bills: Infrastructure costs settled every Monday.
3. Unexpected Expenses: Rare life incidents requiring triage.
4. Debt Pressure: A system that allows survival with a negative balance, introducing psychological and mechanical penalties.

## Debt and Bankruptcy
Being broke is no longer an instant Game Over. Players can enter debt to survive, but long-term critical debt (14+ days) will eventually lead to bankruptcy.
