# Career Level and Professional Identity

## System Identity
The Career Level system tracks the player's long-term professional seniority and social identity within the software industry.

## Persistence
Your career seniority and professional title are part of your persistent profile. Even after closing the browser, your status as a "Reliable Freelancer" or "Industry Veteran" remains intact.

## Career Progression Curve
Leveling up requires exponential EXP: 100 * 1.15^(level-1).

## Career Titles
Titles unlock at level milestones:
- LV 1: Fresh Graduate
- LV 10: Junior Developer
- LV 40: Senior Specialist
- LV 100: Legendary Indie Hacker

## Gaining Career EXP
EXP is earned through professional success:
- Completing contracts successfully.
- Surviving chaos events.
- Milestone achievements.
