# Full Calendar System

## System Identity
The Calendar System replaces abstract day counts with a real, readable calendar. This shift is designed to increase immersion, making the player feel like they are living through actual weeks and months of a freelance career.

## Calendar Configuration
- **Start Date:** Monday, January 4, 2027.
- **Clock:** 24-hour cycle, moving at the Speed Scale defined in the engine.
- **Yearly Progression:** Full support for months and years to track long-term survival.

## Gameplay Impact
- **Weekly Rhythm:** The calendar allows players to internalize the weekly cycle. Mondays are no longer just "Day 8, 15, 22" but are now emotionally recognized as "Bill Day."
- **Career Continuity:** Seeing "March 2027" creates a stronger sense of career longevity than "Day 60."

## UI Display
The Header displays:
- Full weekday and date (e.g., Monday, January 4, 2027).
- Formatted time (e.g., 02:00 AM).
- A small reference to the total days survived.
