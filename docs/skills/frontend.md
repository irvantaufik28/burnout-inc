# Frontend Familiarity

## Skill Identity
Represents the ability to craft intuitive, fast, and visually stunning user interfaces. Covers layout, user experience, and visual polish.

## Gameplay Effects
- **+ Quality:** Directly increases the quality of Consumer Apps and AI Tools.
- **+ Acceptance:** Required for "SaaS Dashboard" and "Landing Page" freelance contracts.
- - **Bugs:** Reduces "UI-related" bug generation in high-progress products.

## Growth Sources
- Building "AI Tools" or "Productivity Apps".
- Completing "UI/UX Design" or "Dashboard" freelance gigs.

## Failure Risks
- Low Quality score at launch.
- "Clunky UI" reviews from users.

## Related Freelance Examples
- "Modern Landing Page" (Frontend 15)
- "Enterprise Admin Dashboard" (Frontend 40, Backend 20)
