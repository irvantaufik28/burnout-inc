# Tech Stack Familiarity System

## Philosophy
Burnout Inc avoids "Excel-style" skill management. We do not track individual frameworks (React, Laravel) because they change too fast and add unnecessary mental load to a management simulation.

Instead, we use **Familiarity Categories**. This abstraction captures the "vibe" of being a specialist while keeping the math simple and the gameplay impactful.

## Goal
The system ensures that:
- Players have a distinct identity (e.g., "The AI Guy" or "Fullstack Generalist").
- Freelance contracts feel different depending on your expertise.
- Building a product becomes more efficient the more you work in that domain.

## How it works
1. **Natural Growth:** You get better at what you actually do. Building an AI tool increases AI familiarity.
2. **Consequences:** Low familiarity leads to more bugs, slower progress, and rejected freelance bids.
3. **Specialization:** Your choice of background (Specialization) acts as a multiplier for certain growth rates.
