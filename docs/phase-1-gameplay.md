# Phase 1: The Fresh Graduate (LV 1–10)

## System Identity
Phase 1 is the onboarding and discovery phase of Burnout Inc. It is designed to expose the player to the breadth of the software industry before they choose a specialization.

## Gameplay Philosophy
- **Exploration over Grinding:** Players should try different types of projects (AI, DevOps, Backend, etc.) rather than just repeating Frontend tasks.
- **Forgiving Pacing:** While deadlines exist, requirements are low enough that most beginners can succeed if they manage their vitals well.
- **Skill Discovery:** Players organically discover which domains they prefer through weighted EXP gain and varied chaos events.

## Starting State
- **Career Level:** 1 (Fresh Graduate).
- **Tech Levels:** All start at Level 2 (Frontend, Backend, AI, DevOps, Mobile).
- **Goal:** Survive the initial weeks of freelance while building a diverse portfolio.

## Emotional Goals
- Feel the "huge" scale of the tech world.
- Experience the first taste of industry stress (Chaos Events).
- Find a "home" in a specific technology domain.

## Progression Rules
- **Diversity Multiplier:** Learning outside the current highest skill gives an EXP bonus.
- **Contract Pool:** A dedicated pool of 15+ beginner projects across all domains.
