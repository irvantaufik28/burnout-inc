# Payment Reminder and Modal System

## System Identity
This system manages the anticipation and resolution of weekly financial obligations. It moves away from silent deductions toward intentional, high-stakes decision-making.

## Reminder Escalation
The Upcoming Bills Panel escalates its visual and psychological pressure as Monday approaches:
- **3-2 Days Left:** Standard reminder.
- **1 Day Left:** Warning state (Orange pulse).
- **Due Tonight:** High-pressure state (Triggers at 18:00 on Sunday).

## Payment Flow
On Monday at 00:00, the game automatically pauses and triggers the **Payment Modal**.
- **Manual Payment:** The player must intentionally click "Pay" to settle invoices.
- **Insufficient Funds:** If the player cannot pay, they are presented with the consequences of an overdue state.

## Consequences of Non-Payment
- **Reputation Damage:** Immediate loss of -15 reputation.
- **Mental Stress:** Immediate "Burnout Risk" condition (48h).
- **Mood Penalty:** Immediate -10 mood loss.
- **Late Fees:** Applied to the next billing cycle.

## Psychological Integration
The system triggers internal monologues ([MIND]) about "almost Monday" and "checking balances," reinforcing the financial anxiety loop.
