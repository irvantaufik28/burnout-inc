# Reputation & Career Progression

## System Identity
Reputation is the "Level" of the freelancer. It dictates the quality of clients and the difficulty of work available.

## Reputation Tiers
- 0-20: Unknown Freelancer
- 20-40: Junior Developer
- 40-60: Reliable Freelancer
- 60-80: Top Rated Expert
- 80-100: Legendary Indie Hacker

## Reputation Sources
- +5 to +15: Successful on-time delivery.
- +2 to +5: Premium Quality delivery.
- +10: Elite contract completion.

## Reputation Penalties
- -10 to -20: Missed deadline.
- -5: Abandoned project.
- -2: Low quality/buggy delivery.

## Gameplay Impact
- Higher Reputation = High % interview success.
- Lower Reputation = Restricted to "Easy" tier contracts.
- Reputation affects the "tier" of randomized contracts generated on the board.
