# Client Archetypes & Preferences

## System Identity
Clients are not just data sources; they are personality profiles that dictate how the player should communicate to succeed.

## Archetype Preferences

### Startup Founder
- **Likes:** Overconfidence, Speed, Ambition ("The Grind").
- **Dislikes:** Extreme caution, Realistic (pessimistic) timelines.

### Enterprise Corp
- **Likes:** Stability, Reliability, Professionalism, Process.
- **Dislikes:** Hype, Risky speed, Meme culture.

### Indie Creator
- **Likes:** Empathy, Friendliness, Passion, Flexibility.
- **Dislikes:** Cold corporate talk, High costs.

### AI Bro Founder
- **Likes:** Automation hype, Optimism, Buzzwords ("Disruption").
- **Dislikes:** Ethics warnings, Slow manual processes.

## Emotional Storytelling
Interviews should reinforce these traits through their feedback.
- "Client was impressed by your raw speed."
- "Client felt you were a bit too 'corporate' for their vibe."
