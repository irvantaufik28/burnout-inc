# Project Condition System

## System Identity
This system adds emotional and mechanical "scars" to projects and the player. It ensures that decisions during chaos events and booster usage have lasting effects on stability and difficulty.

## Core Pillars
- **Gradual Burnout:** Conditions like Burnout Risk apply constant drain rather than instant punishment.
- **Escalation:** Stacking conditions (e.g., Technical Debt + Scope Creep) can turn an easy project into a nightmare.
- **Performance Debt:** Stimulants provide positive conditions that decay into negative aftermaths, forcing a cycle of "borrowed time".

## Condition Categories

### Chaos Conditions
- **Scope Creep:** Slows progress and increases energy drain.
- **Technical Debt:** Decreases efficiency and increases chaos frequency.
- **Client Distrust:** Reduces reputation rewards.

### Booster & Aftermath Conditions
- **Caffeine Rush:** +Speed. Leads to **Focus Crash**.
- **Nicotine Focus:** +Focus stability. Leads to **Energy Drain**.
- **Overclocked:** Massive speed. Leads to **Insomnia**.

### Stimulant Fatigue (Systemic Effects)
- **Overstimulated:** Rapid focus drain and recovery penalties.
- **Anxiety:** High chaos frequency and erratic focus.
- **Focus Instability:** Random fluctuations in auto-work speed.

## Formula Integration
Conditions apply multipliers to progress, drain rates, and chaos probability during each game tick.
