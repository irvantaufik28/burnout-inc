# Freelance System

## System Identity
The survival loop. Provides the necessary capital to cover daily expenses before a product becomes profitable.

## Gameplay Effects
- Gated Access: Higher-paying gigs require specific levels of Tech Familiarity.
- Interviews: Acceptance chance is influenced by current Reputation and Skill levels.
- Trade-off: High rewards usually come with high Energy costs and long durations.

## Contract Examples
- Frontend Landing Page: Requirements: None. Reward: $150.
- Scalable API MVP: Requirements: Backend 15. Reward: $400.
- AI Agent Prototype: Requirements: AI 10, Backend 10. Reward: $350.

## Future Evolution
Later stages will include "Long-term Maintenance" contracts and "Whale" clients that offer higher stability but lower flexibility.
