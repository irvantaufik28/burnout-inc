# Freelance Board and Contract System

## Contract Lifecycle
1. Browse Board.
2. Review Detail (Preview Modal).
3. Send CV.
4. Interview (Event Queue).
5. Delivery & Review (Event Queue).

## Deadline Management
The Task Panel provides high-visibility indicators for deadline proximity. If failure risk is high, a CRITICAL WARNING is displayed, pushing the player toward crunch mode or stimulant abuse.

## Success and Efficiency
Efficiency is heavily modified by vitals and technical match. Low efficiency during critical deadlines creates a high-pressure survival scenario.
