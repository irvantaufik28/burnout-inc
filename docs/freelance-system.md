# Freelance Board & Contract System

## Gameplay Philosophy
The primary source of income and pressure. The system mimics the hustle of real-world freelance platforms (Upwork, etc.) but within a high-stakes simulation environment. 

## Contract Structure
Contracts are randomized objects generated by the system.
- Tiers: Easy, Medium, Hard, Elite.
- Attributes: Title, Reward, Deadline, Client Archetype, Risk Level, Tech Requirements, Reputation Requirement.

## The Loop
1. Search: View randomized varied contracts on the board.
2. Apply: Submit an application (triggers Waiting Flow).
3. Wait: 2-5 seconds delay representing client review.
4. Result: Rejected, Instantly Approved, or Interview Requested.
5. Interview: Complete a lightweight quiz if requested.
6. Execution: Work under deadline to deliver the project.

## Balancing Notes
- Deadlines create tension and force strategic energy management.
- Tech requirements gate higher-tier contracts, encouraging career growth.
- Client types affect deadline strictness and feedback tone.

## Future Scalability
- Long-term maintenance contracts.
- Agency/Team management layers.
