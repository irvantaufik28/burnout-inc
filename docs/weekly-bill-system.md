# Weekly Bill and Invoice System

## System Identity
The Weekly Bill system introduces long-term adulthood anxiety. It creates a recurring financial hurdle settled every Monday at 00:00.

## Payment Flow
Players must settle their invoices through the Payment Modal. If funds are insufficient, the player can choose to enter debt to keep the business operational. Entering debt triggers immediate penalties to reputation and mood, and introduces persistent mechanical debuffs until the balance is cleared.
